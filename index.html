Our Commitment to DEIB

At Automation Framework, we are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive recruitment experience for everyone. We welcome people of all backgrounds, genders, cultures, identities, abilities, and lived experiences to apply. We work hard to make sure every stage, from application to onboarding, is equitable and inclusive.

It’s not just about policies. It’s about how we work, who we hire, and how we actively support diversity in everything we do.

Indigenous Engagement & Economic Participation

  • Prioritize Indigenous Employment: We focus on increasing the representation of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples within our workforce, fostering an inclusive environment and providing direct employment pathways.
  • Offer Partnering and Mentorship: We actively seek partnerships with certified Indigenous businesses for collaboration on projects. We are also dedicated to mentoring Indigenous business owners, sharing our expertise in development, testing, and project management to help them grow and succeed.
  • Prefer Procurement from Indigenous Suppliers: We are committed to directing a portion of our supply chain expenditure towards purchasing products and services from Indigenous-owned businesses, ensuring our procurement practices contribute to the growth of the Indigenous business sector.
